About Chemimi Crumbs

Elegant Parisian-inspired dessert boutique ✦

Chemimi Crumbs is a modern dessert boutique specializing in handcrafted pastries, cakes, and sweet beverages inspired by European elegance and youthful charm.

Every dessert is carefully created to bring comfort, warmth, and a little happiness into everyday life.

At Chemimi Crumbs, desserts are more than just sweet treats. They are tiny moments of comfort shared between friends, families, and loved ones.

Founded with a passion for artisanal baking and aesthetic café culture, Chemimi Crumbs combines premium ingredients, delicate craftsmanship, and dreamy presentation into every menu item we serve.

Our Story

Every dream starts with a tiny spark ✦

Chemimi Crumbs began as a small homemade dessert project created from a simple dream: to make people smile through beautifully crafted sweets.

What started with a few handmade cakes and late-night baking experiments slowly grew into a beloved dessert boutique known for its soft aesthetics and signature flavors.

Today, Chemimi Crumbs continues to create desserts that blend artistry, comfort, and imagination into every bite.

Our shop embraces a warm and elegant atmosphere inspired by Parisian pâtisseries, creating a place where sweetness and memories gently meet.
