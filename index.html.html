About Maison Chemimi

Elegant Parisian-inspired dessert boutique with dreamy pastel aesthetics

Maison Chemimi is a modern dessert boutique specializing in handcrafted pastries, cakes, and sweet beverages inspired by European elegance and youthful charm. Every dessert is carefully created to bring comfort, warmth, and a little happiness into everyday life.

At Maison Chemimi, desserts are more than just sweet treats. They are tiny moments of comfort shared between friends, families, and loved ones.

Founded with a passion for artisanal baking and aesthetic café culture, Maison Chemimi combines premium ingredients, delicate craftsmanship, and dreamy presentation into every menu item we serve.

From soft strawberry shortcakes to rich chocolate éclairs, every creation is designed to feel special, comforting, and unforgettable.

Our Story

Every dream starts with a tiny spark ✦

Maison Chemimi began as a small homemade dessert project created from a simple dream: to make people smile through beautifully crafted sweets.

What started with a few handmade cakes and late-night baking experiments slowly grew into a beloved dessert boutique known for its soft aesthetics and signature flavors.

Today, Maison Chemimi continues to create desserts that blend artistry, comfort, and imagination into every bite.

Our shop embraces a warm and elegant atmosphere inspired by Parisian pâtisseries, creating a place where sweetness and memories gently meet.
